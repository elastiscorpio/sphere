OUR STORY

Mythic Collectibles was born from a passion for fantasy art and tabletop gaming. Our founder, a lifelong collector and artist, wanted to create a world where breathtaking artwork meets immersive gameplay.

Each card in our collection is a masterpiece, illustrated by some of the most talented fantasy artists in the world. We work closely with our artists to ensure every detail captures the essence of the characters and creatures we bring to life.

Beyond cards, we've expanded into high-quality merchandise that lets fans showcase their favorite characters in everyday life. From glow-in-the-dark lithophanes to premium apparel, our products are designed with the same attention to detail as our cards.
